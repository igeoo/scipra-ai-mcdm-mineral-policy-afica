TITLE: Families of slain Marikana mine workers reflect on decade of betrayal

As the country prepares to remember that horrific day 10 years ago, the victims’ families tell Daily Maverick of their sense of betrayal and justice not served. Ntombizolile Mosepetsane, whose husband was killed, says: “It’s like we don’t exist. No one from the government or the mine has come to say sorry.”
